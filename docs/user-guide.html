What Is Nabil Video Studio Pro?

This software takes your raw video and automatically transforms it into a professional, viral-ready YouTube video. It does everything for you:

What You Give It

  • Your raw video file (interview, podcast, sports clip, etc.)
  • Some B-roll clips (optional)

What You Get

  • Professional edited video
  • AI-written viral script
  • Professional voiceover
  • Auto-uploaded to YouTube
  • Thumbnail ideas
Perfect For: Sports channels, news channels, podcast clips, interview compilations, reaction videos, and any content that combines talking + B-roll footage.

Before You Start (One-Time Setup)

You need to set up a few things before using the software. Don't worry, you only do this once!

1
Get a Gemini API Key (FREE)

This is the AI that rewrites your script to make it viral.

  1. Go to https://aistudio.google.com/apikey
  2. Sign in with your Google account
  3. Click "Create API Key"
  4. Copy the key
  5. In the software: Settings > API Keys > Paste your Gemini key
Good News: Gemini has a FREE tier with 60 requests per minute - enough for most users!
2
Create a Fish Audio Account (For Voiceovers)

This creates the professional AI voiceover for your videos.

  1. Go to https://fish.audio/
  2. Create an account
  3. Choose a subscription plan (starts at ~$10/month)
  4. That's it! The software will use your logged-in browser
How It Works: The software opens a browser window and uses your Fish Audio subscription automatically. First time, you'll need to login - after that, it remembers you!
3
Set Up Your Channel (For Auto-Upload)

If you want videos to auto-upload to YouTube:

  1. Go to Settings > Channels
  2. Add your channel name
  3. First upload will ask you to login to YouTube in browser
  4. After that, uploads happen automatically!
Note: Videos upload as UNLISTED. You can review them in YouTube Studio and publish when ready.

Quick Start - Create Your First Video

1
Prepare Your Files

Create a folder with your video inside:

  • Your video file - MP4, MKV, AVI, or MOV
  • Thumbnail image (optional) - Name it like: thumbnail_Your Video Title Here.jpg
2
Open the Software

Launch Nabil Video Studio Pro from your desktop or Start menu.

3
Choose "Create Video"

This is for making a new video from scratch.

4
Select Your Folders
  • Input Folder - Where your video file is
  • B-Roll Folder - Extra clips to use (or leave empty)
  • Output Folder - Where the final video goes
5
Choose Your Channel

Select which channel/profile to use. Each channel has different voice, style, and YouTube account.

6
Click START!

Sit back and relax. The software will:

  1. Analyze your video
  2. Cut it into clips
  3. Write a viral script with AI
  4. Generate professional voiceover
  5. Edit everything together
  6. Upload to YouTube
Time: Depending on video length, this takes 10-30 minutes. You can do other work while waiting!

How It Works (The Magic Behind It)

Here's what happens when you click START:

Step 0: Backup Your Video

Your original video is copied safely, so nothing is lost.

Step 1: Find the Speaker

AI detects when someone is talking in the video and cuts it into clips.

Step 2: Make It Look Professional

Adds frames, backgrounds, and cool animations to the interview clips.

Step 3: Convert Speech to Text

Listens to the video and writes down what people said.

Step 4: AI Writes Viral Script

AI takes the transcript and rewrites it to be engaging and viral!

Step 5: Generate Voiceover

AI creates a professional voice reading the new script.

Step 6-7: Build the Video

Combines voiceover + B-roll clips + music into final video.

Step 8-9: Mix Everything

Alternates between interview clips and voiceover sections for perfect flow.

Step 10: Upload to YouTube

Automatically uploads the video to your YouTube channel (as unlisted).

Bonus: Thumbnail Ideas

AI generates viral thumbnail ideas and prompts you can use!

Common Questions (FAQ)

How much does it cost to run?

Gemini API: FREE (60 requests/minute)
Fish Audio: Starting at ~$10/month for voiceovers
YouTube: Free to upload

What video formats work?

MP4, MKV, AVI, MOV, WMV, FLV, and WEBM. MP4 is recommended.

Can I use my own voice instead of AI?

Yes! Record your own voiceover and put it in the voiceover folder. The software will use it instead.

Why is my video uploading as "Unlisted"?

For safety! This lets you review the video in YouTube Studio before making it public. Just click "Publish" when ready.

The software stopped in the middle. What do I do?

Just run it again! It automatically skips steps that are already done and continues from where it stopped.

How do I change the voice?

Go to Settings > Voices. You can add different Fish Audio voices and assign them to channels.

Can I process multiple videos at once?

Yes! Put multiple videos in different folders (OG-1, OG-2, OG-3...) and select them all. The software processes them in parallel!

Where are my finished videos saved?

In the Output folder you selected. Look for the 9_combined_videos subfolder for the final video.

The browser opened but nothing happened. Why?

You probably need to login to Fish Audio or YouTube. Login in the browser window that opened, and the software will continue automatically.

How do I make the AI script better?

You can customize the AI prompt! Go to Settings > Prompts and edit the template to match your style.
