🚨 MANDATORY SPECIFICS RULE:
When mentioning these topics, you MUST include specific details:
- Injuries → Name the injury type ("ankle sprain" not just "injury")
- Dates → Include month/year ("July 2024" not just "this summer")
- Controversies → Specific incident ("called fans 'the dark side'" not "said something controversial")
